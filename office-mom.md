      <p>Office Mom is your personal wellness companion for macOS that helps you stay healthy and productive throughout your workday. Track your water intake, monitor your daily steps, and maintain healthy sitting/standing intervals with the 20-8-2 rule. With intelligent reminders, real-time menu bar stats, and automatic data archiving, Office Mom keeps you on track without interrupting your workflow.</p>

    <section class="feature-section">
      <h2>Technology</h2>
      <p>Built with <strong>Swift 5.0</strong> and <strong>SwiftUI</strong> for macOS 13.0+, featuring native menu bar integration, <strong>Combine</strong> for reactive state management, and <strong>UserNotifications</strong> for intelligent reminders. Office Mom uses <strong>MVVM architecture</strong> with protocol-oriented design and <strong>async/await</strong> concurrency. All data is stored locally using <strong>UserDefaults</strong> - no external servers, no tracking, complete privacy.</p>
    </section>
